## On the clock

As B.A.H.M.S. employee, you are given the tools to perform your job to the best of your abilities.

### Just Clock In

To start your shift, make sure to clock in. Any clock-in is immediately paid out
in the full [Giga VIP that correspond to your number of clock-ins](/giga-vip/#clock-ins) this season.

Jill may choose to recognize your clock-in and alert Jane your shift started.
Jill is eager to read clock-ins during the beginning of a stream.
She keeps track of major milestones, too!
I wonder what she'll say when one reaches their 100<sup>th</sup> clock-in?

Any clock-ins at the start of the stream are listed off by Jane personally, to
show her appreciation for your shift starting on time.

### Just Clock Out

At the end of your shift, make sure to clock out.
Any clock-outs are rewarded [a nice bonus](/giga-vip/#clock-outs).

Do not clock out before you clock in.
You'll leave precious Giga VIP on the table!

### Just Write a Comment

It's not in Jane's nature to document her code.
It's all perfectly understandable, right?

To help out yourself and your B.A.H.M.S. co-workers,
you can make Jane write a comment in the code by using the "Just Write a Comment" redeem.

Type a fun message to add to the comment.
At some point in time, it will be added to the code.

Misuses might be rejected and refunded.
(Messages that are clearly not meant/suitable as a comment)

* [Best practices for writing code comments](https://stackoverflow.blog/2021/12/23/best-practices-for-writing-code-comments/).

### Just Commit

Let's make sure we actually keep those somewhere safe.
Make Jane commit her code.
Write a fun message that is super relevant to the changes, right?

Commits may happen immediately, or not, but they will happen at some point.

Misuses might be rejected and refunded.
(Messages that are clearly not meant/suitable as a commit)

### Just Suggestions

Did you think of something Jane could add to the bounce house?
Maybe a slight improvement in the code?
You have the power to make it so!

Suggestions follow the format of `[title] description`

```
[Let juniper spin!] Juniper should be able to do a spin/dance
```

The above example is from [an actual suggestion](https://github.com/just-jane-inc/just__stream/issues/250).

Any suggestion will produce a new issue in Jane's [list of GitHub issues](https://github.com/just-jane-inc/just__stream/issues).

Before creating a suggestion, please look for any existing issue that already describes your suggestion.
That would be super helpful.

### Just Word Cloud

Make a personalized word cloud of all your chats!
every chat ever chatted straight out of the database into your warm embrace.

Use [`!discord`](/commands/#info) to invite yourself to the Discord server,
and see your personal art in all its glory.

If you want to highlight specific words in your messages,
use the following format in the redeem message:

```
regex:{expression} color:#RRGGBB
```

| Option               | Description                                                                                                                                                    |
|:---------------------|:---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| `regex:{expression}` | Provide for `expression` a valid [regular expression](https://docs.python.org/3/library/re.html#regular-expression-syntax) between the `{` and `}` delimiters. |
| `color:#RRGGBB`      | Provide an [RGB hexadecimal color notation](https://drafts.csswg.org/css-color/#hex-notation) between `#000000` and `#FFFFFF`.                                 |

As an example, the [following expression](https://regex101.com/r/JMdZQk/1) makes all mentions of `Red Epicness` turn his signature brownish red color.

```
regex:{Red.Epicness} color:#FF4400
```

* https://docs.python.org/3/library/re.html#regular-expression-syntax
* https://regex101.com/
* https://drafts.csswg.org/css-color/#hex-notation

### Just Pyramid Scheme

You want to get in on my [giga-vip downline](/giga-vip/#pyramid-scheme)?

When redeeming the pyramid scheme, you buy in on the next redeem of the pyramid scheme.
Every time the Pyramid Scheme is redeemed,
all the chatters who previously redeemed the scheme will receive 1 Giga VIP, every time.

Once the scheme is redeemed, the cost of the scheme increases according to the formula:

[2<sup>n−1</sup>](https://www.wolframalpha.com/input?i=2%5E%28n%E2%88%921%29+for+n+from+1+to+16)

Where `n` is the number of times the scheme has been redeemed during the current stream.
At the start of the next stream, the cost is reset.

| Redeems | Channel points |
|--------:|---------------:|
|       1 |              1 |
|       2 |              2 |
|       3 |              4 |
|       4 |              8 |
|       5 |             16 |
|       6 |             32 |
|     ... |            ... |

## Custom voice

You may have heard some people using high quality voices with [TTS](/bahms/tts/).
Most likely these are people who have used the [custom voice redeem](#just-let-me-speak).

Custom voices become the persons default and can only be used by them.

### Just Let Me Speak

We add your voice as a [custom TTS voice](/bahms/tts/#custom-voices).

This requires you to submit audio recordings of your own voice.
One of the moderators will get in touch with you after redeeming a custom voice,
to give you specific instructions on how to record your voice.

You will then have the ability to use it, exclusively, for at least 20 streams.
After 20 streams your TTS voice may reset to room for someone else to have their voice customized.
