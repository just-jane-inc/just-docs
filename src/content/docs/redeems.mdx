## On the clock

As B.A.H.M.S. employee, you are given the tools to perform your job to the best
of your abilities.

### Just Clock In

To start your shift, make sure to clock in. Any clock-in is immediately paid out
in the full [Giga VIP that correspond to your number of clock-ins](/giga-vip/#clock-ins). 

Jill may choose to recognize your clock-in and alert Jane your shift started.

Any clock-ins at the start of the stream are listed off by Jane personally, to
show her appreciation for your shift starting on time.

### Just Write a Comment

It's not in Jane's nature to document her code. It's all perfectly
understandable, right?

To help out yourself and your B.A.H.M.S. co-workers, you can make Jane write a
comment in the code by using the "Just Write a Comment" redeem.

Type a fun message to add to the comment. At some point in time, it will be added
to the code.

* [Best practices for writing code comments](https://stackoverflow.blog/2021/12/23/best-practices-for-writing-code-comments/).

### Just Commit

Changes to the code? Let's make sure we actually keep those somewhere safe.
Make Jane commit her code. Write a fun message that is super relevant to the
changes, right?

Commits may happen immediately, or not, but they will happen at some point.

* [Writing commit messages](https://www.chiark.greenend.org.uk/~sgtatham/quasiblog/commit-messages/)
* [`git commit` description](https://git-scm.com/docs/git-commit#_description)

### Just Suggestions

Did you think of something Jane could add to the bounce house? Maybe a slight
improvement in the code? You have the power to make it so!

Suggestions follow the format of `[title] description`

```
[Let juniper spin!] Juniper should be able to do a spin/dance
```

The above example is from [an actual suggestion](https://github.com/just-jane-inc/just__stream/issues/250).

Any suggestion will produce a new issue in Jane's
[list of GitHub issues](https://github.com/just-jane-inc/just__stream/issues).

Before creating a suggestion, please look for any existing issue that already
describes your suggestion. That would be super helpful.

### Just Link

Did you find a cool resource online? Perhaps you want to share a clip of an
adorable bat cat?

By using this redeem, your link will be verified by our highly qualified and
skilled B.A.H.M.S. Uniform Resource Integrity people.

When all is well, your link will be shared in chat, so we can all watch charming
cat girls - I mean, look at the very serious documentation.

### Just Unit Test

Jane promises she will write a
[unit test](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Unit_testing).
She will actually sit through the process of writing a unit test for her code.
You asked for it!

### Just Zshoom

You get a ship in the [bounce house](/bahms/bounce-house/)...
for like some amount of time, maybe.

Here is how you play:

1. Redeem the "Just Zshoom" redeem.
2. Go to [game.bahms.org](https://game.bahms.org/) and log in using your Twitch account.
3. Fly around and shoot as many orbs in the Bounce House as you can.

Once your time is up, your ship will disappear from the Bounce House, and you'll
see your score for that round in chat.

### Just Word Cloud

Make a personalized word cloud of all your chats! every chat ever chatted
straight out of the database into your warm embrace.

Use [`!discord`](/commands/#info) to invite yourself to the Discord server, and
see your personal art in all its glory.

If you want to highlight specific words in your messages, use the following
format in the redeem message:

```
regex:{expression} color:#RRGGBB
```

| Option               | Description                                                                                                                                                    |
|:---------------------|:---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| `regex:{expression}` | Provide for `expression` a valid [regular expression](https://docs.python.org/3/library/re.html#regular-expression-syntax) between the `{` and `}` delimiters. |
| `color:#RRGGBB`      | Provide an [RGB hexadecimal color notation](https://drafts.csswg.org/css-color/#hex-notation) between `#000000` and `#FFFFFF`.                                 |

As an example, the [following expression](https://regex101.com/r/JMdZQk/1) makes
all mentions of `Red Epicness` turn his signature brownish red color.

```
regex:{Red.Epicness} color:#FF4400
```

* https://docs.python.org/3/library/re.html#regular-expression-syntax
* https://regex101.com/
* https://drafts.csswg.org/css-color/#hex-notation

### Just Pyramid Scheme

You want to get in on my [giga-vip downline](/giga-vip/#pyramid-scheme)?

When redeeming the pyramid scheme, you buy in on the next redeem of the pyramid
scheme. Every time the Pyramid Scheme is redeemed, all the chatters who previously
redeemed the scheme will receive 1 Giga VIP, every time.

Once the scheme is redeemed, the cost of the scheme increases according to the
formula:

[2<sup>n−1</sup>](https://www.wolframalpha.com/input?i=2%5E%28n%E2%88%921%29+for+n+from+1+to+16)

Where `n` is the number of times the scheme has been redeemed during the current
stream. At the start of the next stream, the cost is reset.

| Redeems | Channel points |
|--------:|---------------:|
|       1 |              1 |
|       2 |              2 |
|       3 |              4 |
|       4 |              8 |
|       5 |             16 |
|       6 |             32 |
|     ... |            ... |

## Custom voice

You may have heard some people using high quality voices with [TTS](/bahms/tts/).
Most likely these are people who have used the [custom voice redeem](#just-let-me-speak).

Custom voices become the persons default and can only be used by them.

### Just Let Me Speak

We add your voice as a [custom TTS voice](/bahms/tts/#custom-voices).

This requires you to submit audio recordings of your own voice.
One of the moderators will get in touch with you after redeeming a custom voice,
to give you specific instructions on how to record your voice.

You will then have the ability to use it, exclusively, for at least 20 streams.
After 20 streams your TTS voice may reset to room for someone else to have their
voice customized.
