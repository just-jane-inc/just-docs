## Battle Phases

To catch a fish, you must win against the fish across three challenges.
Just like in real fishing.

These steps will play out in real time before your very eyes:

<Steps>
    1. **Lure:** First and foremost, the lure must be alluring to the fish.
       Fish have varied taste and some fish are more cunning than others.
    2. **Hook:** An interested fish will take a bite, but it might escape the
       hook if it succeeds its saving though.
    3. **Line:** This is where the real battle begins.
       A test of strength and stamina occurs until either the fish gets away, or
       you reel it in.
</Steps>

Complete all three steps and you got yourself a new fish!

## Rods

![Fishing rod example](../../../assets/fishing-rod-example.png "Example of a freshly crafted uncommon fishing rod with 12 hook, 15 lure and 17 line")

After purchasing a rod, it will be revealed on screen.
Some rods are better than others.
Rods have several attributes that are randomly assigned.
The example above shows a freshly crafted uncommon fishing rod with 12 hook, 15 lure and 17 line.  

<MiddleDot /> **Rarity:** Rods can be common, uncommon, rare, epic, or legendary.
Rarity gives an attribute bonus to hook, lure and line.  
<MiddleDot color="#E62937"/> **Hook:** How likely you'll hook a speedy fish.  
<MiddleDot color="#FDF900"/> **Lure:** How likely you'll lure a cunning fish.  
<MiddleDot color="#DEADFF"/> **Line:** How easily a fish will lose stamina.
