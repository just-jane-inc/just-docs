Juniper is a cute tamagotchi-like hedgehog.

Currently, she can be patted, fed and hydrated, with future plans for deeper integrations with our [redeems](/redeems).

She is in active development and her code is available [on GitHub](https://github.com/just-jane-inc/just-juniper).
