You want to get in on my [giga-vip downline](/giga-vip/#pyramid-scheme)?

When redeeming the pyramid scheme, you buy in on the next redeem of the pyramid scheme.
Every time the Pyramid Scheme is redeemed,
all the chatters who previously redeemed the scheme will receive 1 Giga VIP, every time.

Once the scheme is redeemed, the cost of the scheme increases according to the formula:

[2<sup>n−1</sup>](https://www.wolframalpha.com/input?i=2%5E%28n%E2%88%921%29+for+n+from+1+to+16)

Where `n` is the number of times the scheme has been redeemed during the current stream.
At the start of the next stream, the cost is reset.
